01 · High-value business problem

Supplier selection is slow, inconsistent, and operationally risky

Industrial procurement teams compare price, delivery, quality, reliability, financial risk, ESG, and commercial terms across emails and spreadsheets. Manual review is hard to reproduce and can favor the lowest price even when a late supplier threatens production. Solving this protects spend, continuity, compliance, and auditability.

05 · Impact and estimated ROI

Faster analysis with visible sourcing value

The demonstrated recommendation has a USD 3,600 scenario cost advantage versus the next-best on-time supplier and remains USD 4,800 under budget. A conservative planning case of ten similar requests per month estimates analysis time falling from 2.5 hours to 20 minutes, producing USD 7,812 in annual labor capacity and USD 27,120 in sourcing benefit.

Estimated annual benefit = $7,812 + $27,120 = $34,932
Illustrative cost = $5,000/year
ROI = ($34,932 − $5,000) / $5,000 = 599%
Estimated payback ≈ 1.7 months
